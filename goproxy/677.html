火狐一般有两个必须的手动修改
开启流水线(pipelining)
network.http.pipelining 和 network.http.proxy.pipelining 值为 true，更改 network.http.pipelining.maxrequests 值为8 [如果经常遇见"疑似DDOS保护测试", 调小这个数值.]

WebRTC泄漏
“media.peerconnection.enabled”, double click to set it to false.
https://ipleak.net/
